Fried Mozzarella Balls

Vitamin E acts as an antioxidant, neutralizing unstable molecules that can damage cells. Diets rich in vitamin E may help prevent Alzheimer's disease

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Mozzarella
  • 5 cup Vegetable oil
  • 1 cup Bread crumb
  • 3 extra large Egg
  • 1 can Tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Separate mozzarella and form into 8-10 small balls.
  2. Heat about 1-1/2-inches oil to 360F in a 3 1/2-to 4-quart heavy saucepan.
  3. Meanwhile, double-coat mozzarella balls by dipping in eggs, then in breadcrumbs, and repeating.
  4. Transfer to a sheet of wax paper.
  5. Working in small batches, lower balls into oil with a slotted spoon and fry, turning occasionally, until golden brown, about 30 seconds per batch. Transfer to paper towels to drain and season with salt. (Return oil to 360F between batches.)
