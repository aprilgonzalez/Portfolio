Broccoli Parmesan Pasta

This pasta is fast and easy enough to make in less than 20 minutes and 5 ingredients in your fridge. It is delicious, filling, and a healthy dinner. It is great to bring your loved ones together to dine with you.It also has a unique anti-inflammatory health benefits

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta, uncooked
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese, vegan parmesan substitute, or nutritional yeast
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring 6 cups water to a boil in a pot
  2. Cook pasta according to directions on package
  3. Bring 1-2 inches of water to a boil in a small saucepan
  4. Chop broccoli into even pieces and place in a steamer basket and place on top of the saucepan
  5. Cover with lid and steam until tender, about 5-6 minutes
  6. Alternately, cook broccoli covered in the microwave for 3-5 minutes, or until tender
  7. Drain cooked pasta and return to pan
  8. Add olive oil, cooked broccoli, grated Parmesan, and salt and pepper to taste

Papaya Strawberry Smoothie

Smoothies contains nutrients that your body needs, especially papaya and strawberry. Papaya contains a rich antioxidants, good source of fiber, and etc. Strawberry contains minerals and antioxidants as well. Both papaya and strawberry combined together is added tons of nutrients and antioxidants.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup papaya, peeled, seeded and chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup LACTAID¬Æ Reduced Fat Milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey, or as needed
  • 3 large ice cubes
  • Papaya or strawberry slices (optional)
  • Fresh mint (optional)

Directions

  1. In a blender container combine papaya, strawberries, reduced fat milk, and honey
  2. Cover and blend until smooth
  3. With the blender running, add ice cubes, one at a time, through the opening in the lid, until ice is crushed and mixture is smooth
  4. Pour into glasses. If desired, garnish with additional strawberries or sliced papaya and fresh mint
  5. Serve immediately

Scrambled Egg Burritos

This scrambled egg burritos is perfect a sunny morning and is great to start off the morning with a nice, and warm, filling scrambled egg burritos.It is healthy, high proteins, and a low fat breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs, or equivalent egg substitute
  • 1 tablespoon 1% low-fat milk
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Dash of coarsely ground black pepper
  • Cooking spray
  • 1/2 teaspoon butter
  • 4 tablespoons reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 2 (8-inch) fat-free flour tortillas, heated
  • 4 tablespoons chopped seeded tomato, divided
  • 2 tablespoons bottled chunky salsa, divided

Directions

  1. Whisk the first 5 ingredients (through pepper) in a medium bowl
  2. Coat a medium nonstick skillet with cooking spray, and melt butter over medium heat
  3. Add the egg mixture, and stir with a heatproof rubber spatula to scramble
  4. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons cheese down the center of one tortilla; top with half of the scrambled egg, 2 tablespoons tomato, and 1 tablespoon chunky salsa
  5. Roll up burrito-style (fold bottom up and sides to center)
  6. Repeat with remaining ingredients

Maple Glazed Salmon

This salmon is not just an ordinary salmon, it contains a sweet flavor added on. It is delicious and filling for a nice evening dinner.Eating fish is general can reduce your risk of dying from heart attack. Maple glazed adds a light texture and sweetness to the fish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 2 (6-ounce) salmon fillets (about 1 inch thick)
  • Cooking spray

Directions

  1. Preheat Broiler
  2. Combine first 4 ingredients in a small bowl; stir with a whisk
  3. Place salmon, skin side down, on a broiler pan coated with cooking spray
  4. Brush with maple mixture
  5. Broil 10 to 12 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork, brushing with maple mixture after 5 minutes and again after 10 minutes

This salad is great for the sunny summertime, it is very healthy and a filling lunch.It‚Äôs a perfect mixture for a sweet and savory fruits and vegetable. It is also high in protein and easily digested.

Ingredients

  • 400g tin chickpeas in water, drained

  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • 200g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 small courgettes, sliced into ribbons with a vegetable peeler
  • 50g peppadew peppers

Directions

Directions

  2. Add all the remaining ingredients apart from the fresh coriander and pumpkin seeds
  3. Season with ground black pepper and cover with 1 litre boiling water
  4. Bring back up to the boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20‚Äì25 min until the veg are tender
  5. Remove the pan from the heat, then blend with a stick blender until smooth
  6. Divide the soup among 6 bowls, then sprinkle over the coriander, pumpkin seeds and some black pepper to serve

Whole Grain Raspberry Jam Bars

These homemade bars are great for on-the-go breakfast and also for an energy booster snack. It contains a low calorie and good source of whole wheat..This breakfast bar is filled with fiber and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil spray
  • 2 cups whole-wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup wheat germ
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 sticks butter, cut into 16 pieces and softened
  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup walnut pieces
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground flax seeds
  • 3/4 cup sugar-free raspberry jam
  • 3/4 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞ F. Line a 13 x 9-inch pan with foil by spreading two sheets of foil perpendicular to each other over the pan, with extra foil hanging over the edges
  2. Grease the foil with cooking spray
  3. Whisk the flour, wheat germ, granulated sugar and salt in a large bowl
  4. Beat in 14 tablespoons of butter with an electric mixer on low until the mixture looks like wet sand, about 1¬Ω minutes
  5. Reserve 1¬º cups of the mixture for the topping and put the rest into the pan. Use the bottom of a measuring cup to press it into an even layer
  6. Bake the crust until the edges begin to brown, about 14-18 minutes
  7. Mix the reserved flour mixture with the oats, nuts, brown sugar and flax seeds
  8. Add the remaining butter and pinch the mixture with your fingers into small clumps
  9. In a separate bowl, lightly smash the raspberries, jam and lemon juice together
  10. Spread the berry mixture over the hot crust and sprinkle the crumb streusel on top
  11. Bake until golden brown, about 22-25 minutes
  12. Let the bars cool completely in the pan
  13. Remove bars from pan by lifting up the foil edges. Cut into 24 squares
